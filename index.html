About Our Organization

Dedicated to Justice, Awareness, and Public Welfare

Welcome to Chamunda Grahak Suraksha Mandal

Founded on the principles of social service and justice, Chamunda Grahak Suraksha Mandal (CGSM) is a non-governmental organization committed to safeguarding consumer rights and promoting public welfare. We operate with a firm belief that an informed consumer is an empowered citizen.

Our work encompasses a wide range of activities, from providing crucial legal guidance to consumers facing exploitation, to conducting extensive consumer education and awareness campaigns across urban and rural communities. We strive to build a fair and transparent marketplace where the rights of every individual are respected and protected.

Our Core Objectives

Our efforts are directed towards achieving the following goals for a better society.

Consumer Awareness

To educate consumers about their rights and responsibilities through workshops, seminars, and public campaigns.

Consumer Protection

To protect consumers from unfair trade practices, misleading advertisements, and defective goods or services.

Legal Assistance

To offer guidance and support to consumers in filing complaints and seeking redressal in consumer courts.

Public Education

To integrate consumer education into formal and non-formal education systems to empower future generations.

Know Your Consumer Rights

Empower yourself with the knowledge of fundamental rights granted by the Consumer Protection Act.

Right to Safety

Protection from goods and services that are hazardous to life and property.

Right to Information

The right to be informed about the quality, quantity, purity, standard, and price of goods.

Right to Choose

The right to be assured, wherever possible, access to a variety of goods and services at competitive prices.
