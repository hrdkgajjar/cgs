Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and consumer rights.

We provide free guidance and assistance for a wide range of consumer issues, including defective products, poor services, unfair trade practices, and misleading advertisements. We help you understand your rights and guide you on how to file a complaint in the appropriate consumer forum.

Our primary guidance and consultation services are free of charge. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to public welfare. However, for specific legal procedures or documentation, there might be nominal actual costs involved, which will be communicated to you transparently.

You can become a member by contacting our office directly via phone or email. Please visit our Membership Page for more details on the benefits and the process to join our cause.

While our office is based in Ahmedabad, we provide guidance to consumers across Gujarat. For issues outside the state, we can help connect you with relevant organizations and resources in your area.
