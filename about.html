About Our Organization

Championing Consumer Rights and Public Welfare Since Our Inception

Our Story: A Commitment to Justice

Chamunda Grahak Suraksha Mandal (CGSM) was established with the core belief that a just society is built upon the foundation of empowered and aware citizens. For years, we have been at the forefront of the consumer rights movement in Gujarat, evolving from a small group of passionate social workers into a leading non-governmental organization.

Our journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of fairness in the marketplace. We provide a vital platform for individuals to voice their grievances, seek legal guidance, and understand their rights and responsibilities. Through consumer education, social service, and unwavering advocacy, we work to bridge the gap between consumers and service providers, ensuring transparency and accountability for all.

Our Mission

  • To educate and empower every citizen with comprehensive knowledge of their consumer rights.
  • To provide accessible and effective legal guidance to victims of consumer fraud and unfair trade practices.
  • To advocate for stronger consumer protection policies and their transparent implementation.
  • To foster a culture of social responsibility and ethical conduct in the marketplace.

Our Vision

To create an alert, responsible, and empowered consumer society in India, where every citizen can confidently participate in the marketplace, free from the fear of exploitation, and where justice is swift, accessible, and equitable for all.

Our Core Objectives

Our actions are guided by a clear set of objectives aimed at creating tangible, positive change.

Consumer Awareness

To design and execute impactful awareness campaigns, workshops, and seminars that educate consumers about their rights, responsibilities, and the available redressal mechanisms.

Consumer Protection

To actively protect consumers against unfair trade practices, restrictive trade policies, misleading advertisements, and the sale of hazardous goods and services.

Legal Assistance

To offer expert guidance and procedural support to consumers for filing and pursuing complaints in the appropriate consumer forums and commissions.

Public Education

To collaborate with educational institutions to introduce and promote consumer education, nurturing a generation of informed and vigilant citizens.

Complaint Guidance

To serve as a primary resource for consumers, guiding them through the entire process of grievance redressal, from documentation to representation.

Social Responsibility

To undertake various social service initiatives, including women empowerment programs and rural outreach, to ensure inclusive growth and welfare.

Consumer Advocacy

To represent the collective interests of consumers at policy-making forums and advocate for legislative changes that strengthen the consumer protection framework.

Fair Trade Promotion

To encourage and promote ethical business practices and fair trade, fostering a healthy and competitive economic environment for the benefit of all stakeholders.

Chamunda Grahak Suraksha Mandal
