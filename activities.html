Our Activities

Engaging communities and driving change through a variety of programs and initiatives.

Consumer Awareness Camps

We regularly organize camps in urban and rural areas to educate the public about their rights, new regulations, and how to avoid common scams.

Seminars & Workshops

Hosting seminars with legal experts, government officials, and industry leaders to discuss pressing consumer issues and policy changes.

Legal Consultation

Providing free, first-level legal advice and guidance to consumers who have been wronged, helping them prepare and file complaints.

School & Youth Awareness

Conducting interactive sessions in schools and colleges to empower the next generation of consumers with knowledge and critical thinking skills.

Women Empowerment

Specialized programs focused on educating women about their consumer rights, financial literacy, and avenues for economic independence.

Rural Consumer Education

Targeted outreach initiatives to ensure that consumers in remote and rural areas are not left behind and have access to information and support.
